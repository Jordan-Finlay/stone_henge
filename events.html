Now he is turning his attention to Stonehenge. Dr. Watson has been reading up on the origins of the monument but will they be able to solve the 'Mystery of the Stones'?

FESTIVAL OF NEOLITHIC IDEAS

Come along to our very first Festival of Neolithic Ideas on 11th and 12th November and get involved with an outstanding programme of hands-on activities, demonstrations, talks and tours, led by experts in the field. From radiocarbon dating and ancient DNA, to astronomy and laser scanning, we will reveal how the latest advances in science and archaeological techniques have developed our understanding of the Neolithic period, including Stonehenge and the people who built it. The festival will also explore the tools and technologies used by Neolithic people to understand and shape the world around them.
